Case Studies.

Sana - Wellness App

Sana is a food delivery app. Sana was created in order to cover the needs of the people that don't have enough time to cook healthy but they need to eat healthy to have a good health condition. The idea was to provide users a healthier and more customized food delivery options.

Iceland - Editorial Project

The website provides information about Iceland touristic places. The core of the website is nature. In the research process the main reason that tourists visit Iceland is the nature of the country, which is why this editorial was created to provide information about the main geographical features.

About me.

I'm a UX/UI designer based in Madrid with multidisciplinar background with artistic and management skills. I started working for 8 years for the audiovisual media as a make-up artist working for TV channels such as TVE, La Sexta, Castilla La Mancha Media…

After acquiring experience in this industry and developing enthusiasm about other fields related to this medium, my willingness to learn new things led me to study photography and graphic design, which I practiced for a while, combining it with the management of a small business starting with a team of 2 people until expanding it to a 4 people team working horizontally.

My motivation is to be able to apply all my knowledge developed during these years in this new stage as UX/UI Designer in order to continue acquiring new knowledge and continue developing my creative, visual and design skills.
